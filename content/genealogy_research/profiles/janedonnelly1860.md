## Birth & Emigration to New Zealand
Jane Donnelly was born on the 11th of August in 1869 in Bendigo, Victoria, Australia, to parents James Donnelly and Kate Campbell Donnelly (n&eacute;e King).[^1] Her parents had emigrated to Australia from Ireland: Jane's mother Kate came from Ballycraigie in Northern Ireland and Jane's father James came from Rockcorry, in the Republic of Ireland. James and Kate met and married in Australia in the goldfields of Bendigo where they started their family before moving to Naseby, Otago, New Zealand, where they settled and continued their family.
[^1]: The Victorian Registry of Births, Deaths, and Marriages; Melbourne, Victoria, Australia; Victoria, Australia, Birth Records. Name: Jane Donnelly; Birth Reg Date: 1861; Birth Reg Place: Victoria Australia; Father: James Donnelly; Mother Maiden Name: King: Ref Number: 10476.

## Death
Jane Paisley died on the 3rd of February in 1912 at the age of 54 [^41] from a combination of shock and burns received when her clothing accidentally caught fire while she was attending a bonfire in her back yard [^42]: 
[^41]: Jane's death record can be ordered from [BDM Historical Records](https://www.bdmhistoricalrecords.dia.govt.nz/search) - death registration # 1912/466.
[^42]: Jane's death was reported in many papers: https://paperspast.natlib.govt.nz/newspapers/MIC19120209.2.14.4 Mount Ida Chronicle, Volume XL, Issue XL, 9 February 1912, Page 3; https://paperspast.natlib.govt.nz/newspapers/ODT19120206.2.16 Otago Daily Times, Issue 15370, 6 February 1912, Page 3; https://paperspast.natlib.govt.nz/newspapers/ESD19120206.2.25 Evening Star, Issue 14792, 6 February 1912, Page 4; https://paperspast.natlib.govt.nz/newspapers/OW19120207.2.214 Otago Witness, Issue 3021, 7 February 1912, Page 63 - Papers Past, the National Library of New Zealand.
